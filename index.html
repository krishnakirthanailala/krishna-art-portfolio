About

From a young age, I found myself captivated by the way structures shape the world around us, leading me to explore architecture not just as a field of study, but as a medium for creativity and problem-solving. Alongside this, my passion for art has allowed me to translate ideas into visual forms, blending imagination with precision. Numbers and patterns have always come naturally to me, and my aptitude in mathematics fuels my analytical thinking, allowing me to approach challenges with logic, strategy, and a touch of creativity.

Driven by a relentless pursuit of perfection, I constantly refine my skills, whether sketching a building concept, experimenting with design, or tackling complex mathematical problems. Each project is an opportunity to push boundaries, learn from mistakes, and transform abstract ideas into tangible outcomes. My ultimate dream is to attend MIT, where I hope to immerse myself in an environment that nurtures innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence, and to contribute meaningfully to the worlds of architecture, design, and STEM.

Through my work, I strive to balance creativity and precision, imagination and discipline, always aiming to create designs and solutions that are thoughtful, purposeful, and impactful.
